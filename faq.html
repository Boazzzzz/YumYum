Where is your service offered ?

Where is the food prepared ?

We have our own kitchens located across the cities.

I have an allergy, am gluten-free or have a specific restriction. Can you accommadate ?

absolutely! this is why we exist- to offer delicious healthy meals no matter what! we have experience cooking for whole30, vegan, gluten free, dairy free, low fodmap, paleo, ketogenic diets and more. many of our clients have other specific dietary needs which we are happy to accommodate.

Where is the cancelation policy ?

we have a 48 hour cancellation policy. without 48 hours notice, the service fee will still apply. if you need to skip a week due to travel or other obligations, please inform us of any dates as far in advance as possible with at least 48 hours notice.

Do you cook for babies ?

absolutely! we love cooking for the whole family! we love helping your picky eaters try new vegetables in creative ways or creating homemade meals or purees for your growing baby.
